🌾 Rice Varieties & Benefits

கருப்புக் கவுணி (Black Kavuni)

  • ✔ Rich in antioxidants
  • ✔ Helps control diabetes
  • ✔ Improves heart health
  • ✔ Supports weight management

மாப்பிள்ளை சம்பா (Mappillai Samba)

  • ✔ Increases stamina and strength
  • ✔ High iron content
  • ✔ Improves bone health
  • ✔ Boosts energy recovery

இரத்தசாலி (Rathasali)

  • ✔ Improves blood health
  • ✔ Boosts immunity
  • ✔ Good for skin health
  • ✔ Cooling effect for body

பூங்கார் (Poongar)

  • ✔ Good for women’s health
  • ✔ Supports reproductive health
  • ✔ High fiber content
  • ✔ Improves digestion

தூயமல்லி (Thooyamalli)

  • ✔ Easy to digest
  • ✔ Light for stomach
  • ✔ Suitable for daily use
  • ✔ Helps maintain weight

ஆ.கி. சம்பா (A.K. Samba)

  • ✔ Good energy source
  • ✔ Supports digestion
  • ✔ Traditional healthy rice
  • ✔ Low glycemic impact

சீரக சம்பா (Seeraga Samba)

  • ✔ Aromatic rice variety
  • ✔ Improves digestion
  • ✔ Low sugar spike
  • ✔ Best for biryani & daily meals
